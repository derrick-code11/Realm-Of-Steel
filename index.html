Instructions:

Welcome to Realm of Steel! Get ready for epic battles and intense action. Here's how to play:

Controls:

  • Ember(Red Samurai):

    • Move Up: W
    • Move Left: A
    • Move Right: D
    • Basic Attack: Space Bar

  • Xander(Blue Samurai)

    • Move Up: Up Arrow
    • Move Left: Left Arrow
    • Move Right: Right Arrow
    • Basic Attack: Enter
